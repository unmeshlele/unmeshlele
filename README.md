### 🏗️ Building now — Zaltt

**[Zaltt](https://www.zaltt.com/)** trains a private AI model on a firm's own portfolio and signature style, turning a brief, an exported view, or a sketch into a client-ready render. The Enterprise tier runs on a workstation inside the office: air-gapped, on the firm's own hardware, nothing sent to any cloud, so sensitive designs and IP never leave the building.

As Founder & Head of Product I own discovery, roadmap, UX, and delivery across a React/TypeScript frontend and a FastAPI backend wrapping a local render engine (ComfyUI, FLUX, LoRA, ControlNet) plus a local LLM.

> *"AI built by an architect. Because software engineers don't know what a curtain wall is."*

### About

I'm an architect turned designer. Six years drawing buildings before I drew a single screen, and the job turned out to be the same: find the one idea holding everything up, then quietly remove the rest. These days I design **and ship** React products end to end, where **AI, behaviour, and complex systems** meet.

- **Ship end-to-end** — discovery and research → interaction design → React/TypeScript build
- **Service & interaction design** — org-level blueprinting down to detailed screen flows
- **Behavioural design** — behavioural economics and gamification to lift engagement
- **Systems thinking** from a computational-architecture background (Rhino, Grasshopper)
